How To Play

  • Your goal is to build the atom shown in the Element Card
  • Add protons and neutrons to the nucleus
  • Add electrons to the electron shells (inner shell first)
  • Click "Check Atom" to verify if you've built the correct atom
  • Protons determine the element identity
  • Neutrons determine the isotope
  • Electrons must equal protons for a neutral atom
