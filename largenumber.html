Understanding Large Numbers

Numbers help us count, measure, and compare things. When numbers get bigger, we need special ways to read and understand them.

In the Indian numbering system, we use terms like Lakh (1,00,000) and Crore (1,00,00,000) to make large numbers easier to read.

5-Digit Numbers

10,000 to 99,999 are five-digit numbers.

Example: 45,321 is forty-five thousand three hundred twenty-one.

Lakh (1,00,000)

1 Lakh = 100,000 = One hundred thousand

Example: 3,50,000 is three lakh fifty thousand.

Crore (1,00,00,000)

1 Crore = 10,000,000 = Ten million

Example: 2,30,00,000 is two crore thirty lakh.

Visualizing 1 Lakh

Imagine each coin represents 10,000. How many coins make 1 Lakh?

10 coins × 10,000 = 1,00,000 (1 Lakh)
